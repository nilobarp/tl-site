Trade Ledger operates a low cost, highly innovative, high volume platform model.

We create innovate new digital lending products and operations for our lending customers, based on superior B2B trading information. But the real magic is re-imagining the lending customer experience. From the outset we decided that addressing the asymmetry problem required a new way of thinking about the user experience.

Why does it take 30 hours of work for your customer, mostly reformatting existing digital financial and business information, to apply for a cash flow loan? We decided we needed to address the customer experience end-to-end in order to create a delightful experience.
